## APPENDIX A: DATA COLLECTION INSTRUMENTS

### I. Interview Guide for Management
The following questions were used during the qualitative data collection phase to understand the operational challenges of B Special Business Ltd.

1. How is inventory currently tracked across the warehouse and retail branches?
2. What are the most common causes of data discrepancies in your current ledger system?
3. How long does it typically take to reconcile monthly sales against physical stock?
4. What security measures are currently in place to authorize stock movements between locations?
5. How do you forecast which products need restocking for the upcoming week?

### II. Observation Checklist
During the field study, the following elements were observed and recorded:
* [ ] Frequency of manual data entry errors in the paper ledgers.
* [ ] Time taken to process a single customer sale using manual receipts.
* [ ] Verification process for incoming stock at the retail branch.
* [ ] Physical condition and organization of the central warehouse.
